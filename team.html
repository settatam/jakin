Our Team

Board of Directors

MR SEYI WRIGHT (BOARD CHAIRMAN)

Mr Oluseyi Wright is the Chief Executive Officer of L&V, has conducted over 100 Management Development seminars, co-ordinated a number of consulting projects and has over 28 years working experience. A significant part of Seyi’s experience was in the banking industry. He was until April 1997, Chief Executive Officer of Pacific Merchant Bank. Before then, he had worked with Chase Merchant Bank, Lagos (an affiliate of Chase Manhattan Bank New York), which later changed to Continental Merchant Bank.

He was at various times the head of the Corporate Banking Division and Capital Markets Division of the Bank. Seyi holds a Masters Degree from the University of Ibadan, and has attended several management training programs including the International Executive Program at IMD, Switzerland and the Executive Internship Program in International Banking at American Express Bank Limited, New York.

MRS ALERO OTOBO

Alero is a graduate of Oxford University with a Sloan Masters degree in Management from the London Business School. Aleroâ€™s strength is her diverse career experience in the public and private sector. She started her career in the Public Service serving in the Federal Ministries of Works and Finance and thereafter spent 14 years in the Financial Services Industry where she worked in Treasury, Product Management and HR.

Alero has invaluable understanding of Sector-wide reforms in Nigeria. She is passionate about Change and transforming individuals and organizations. She currently serves on the International Alumni Board of the London Business School, the Central Working Committee of the Apostles in the Market Place and is the Vice President of the International Christian Chamber of Commerce (Nigeria).

She is also a member of the Human Capital Development Policy Commission of the National Economic Summit Group. And she is also serving as the coordinator of the Education Reform Team and CEO of Incubator Africa.

MRS ABOLAJI OSIME (BOARD VICE CHAIRMAN)

Abolaji is the CEO & Founder of Global International College, a Secondary & Sixth form College with branches in Lagos & Abuja and representative offices in Ibadan & Port Harcourt. Global International College is one of the leading providers of secondary, university prep and foundation degrees for international universities in Nigeria. She is also the Lead Consultant of Strategic Transformation Team Limited, a premier educational consultancy with a wide range of experience and expertise in public and private education sector reform.

She consulted for DFID ESSPIN programme as the Lagos State Team Leader focusing on the following priorities MTSS/ESP ,Quality Assurance, Private School Strategy, Teacher Training, EMIS, Communication & Knowledge Management, Budget Tracking, Monitoring Learning Achievement ,School Transformation. She developed a private education strategy for Lagos State for private education sector reform.

Focusing mainly on private schools for the poor she consulted for DFID in the production of a report and study on the private school regulatory frame work in Lagos and later consulted on the development of a framework on the political economy of low cost private schools in Lagos for a proposed intervention by DFID. She has consulted for several State Governments developing and strategies for a holistic reform of public and private education delivery including technical and vocational education Abolaji is also an independent consultant to the Federal Ministry of Education (Nigeria), she's a member of the Education Reform Team - an independent platform for education specialists committed to the transformation of Nigeria, through education.

MRS ADEBIYI OLUBUKOLA (BOARD SECRETARY)

MRS ADEBIYI OLUBUKOLA nee Akinlosotu obtained a B.Sc degree in Management and Accounting from Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU) in 2002. In 2008, she obtained an EDS Certificate in Entrepreneurial Management (CEM) from Pan African University (LBS) while in 2013 she obtained an Executive Masters Course Certificate in Project Management from the British School of Project Management.

She worked briefly in two organizations before launching off on her own on February 1st 2004 to start Just J Ventures, a supply company. Since October 2004, young but naturally endowed with a strong leadership and entrepreneurial spirit, her passion for God and huge burden for the less privileged led her into establishing and managing Jakinminis International Company limited by guarantee (JAKIN N.G.O), where she serves as the President/Chief Executive Officer .

So far, JAKIN has assisted over 50,000 less priviledged people in Nigeria and as far as Liberia, Sudan, Darfur and Chad. Her zeal and commitment earned her three humanitarian service awards. In 2008, she won a one year Scholarship under the Goldman Sachs Bank (USA) 10,000 women initiative to empower 10,000 women entrepreneurs in emerging and developing countries across the world. Mrs Adebiyi is a resound motivational speaker and facilitator.

She is a lover of children and a mentor to hundreds of youths and women. She is happily married to Dr Olubunmi Adebiyi and blessed with son.

DR EYITAYO OLUBUNMI ADEBIYI

DR EYITAYO OLUBUNMI ADEBIYI, is the Co-president of Jakinminis International Company Ltd/Gte. He qualified as a Medical Doctor from the University of Lagos in 1998 and practiced for three years. His passion for humanitarian service led him into obtaining his masters of public health (MPH) from the same university of Lagos in 2004.

He started his career in public health as the Zonal Health Officer (SW) of the Nigerian Red Cross Society. After almost three years, he became the Senior Medical Services Advisor (Edo Zone) on HIV/AIDS management for Family Health International (GHAIN Project) where he worked for four years before proceeding to Hygeia Foundation as part of the senior management team in 2008.

He obtained a second Masters degree in Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Benin in 2008. He is a certified Program Manager and a Healthcare development consultant. With his experience in both local and International organizations, he facilitates in various public health workshops and seminars for companies and organizations. He is happily married to Olubukola Adebiyi.

MR KINGSLEY OMOSE

Kingsley Omose is the Managing Partner of Reliance Partners, a firm of Legal practitioners based in Apapa, Lagos. In 1986, he graduated from University of Benin with a Bachelors degree in Law and from the Nigerian Law School in 1987. He immediately started his career in Law at Shetimah Liberty & Co before proceeding 4yrs after to join Abogah & Co in 1990 where he worked for another 5yrs.

Now with almost 10yrs experience in the law practice, he boldly stepped out in 1995 to set up his own firm called Omose & Co which he managed diligently for 7yrs before establishing Reliance Partners in 2006.

Though with 22yrs working experience in the Law profession, he still writes as a hobby with a view to enhancing a common way of viewing, interpreting and relating to issues. His articles have been published in various National Dailies such as Businessday, Punch, The Sun, The Guardian etc. He is married to Olubunmi and they have 3 children.
