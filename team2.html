Our Team

Board of Directors

MRS ALERO OTOBO holds a first degree in Human Sciences from Oxford University, a Master in Philosophy Degree in Town and country Planning from University of London, and a Sloan Masters in Management from the prestigious London Business School.

She was Head of Human Capital Management in United Bank for Africa (UBA) Plc., and has served on the Board of Union Bank of Nigeria as a non Executive Director . She currently serves on the International Alumni Board of the London Business School. She is a Human Resource Consultant with expertise in Human Resource Strategy, Organization design, Change Management and Executive Coaching.

MR SEYI WRIGHT has over 29 years working experience, a significant part of which was in the banking industry. He was until April 1997, Chief Executive Officer of Pacific Merchant Bank. Before then, he had worked with Chase Merchant Bank, Lagos (an affiliate of Chase Manhattan Bank New York).

Author of the book Choose to Make A Difference, he was at various times the head of the Corporate Banking Division and Capital Markets Division of the Bank. Seyi has attended several management training programs including the Chase Merchant Bank Credit Training Programme, International Executive Program at IMD, Switzerland and the Executive Internship Programme in International Banking at American Express Bank Limited, New York. He is presently the Chief Executive Officer of Leadership & Vision Ltd. With significant practical leadership exposure and excellent strategy consulting experience.

MRS ABOLAJI OSIME obtained a law degree from the University of London in 1984 and a postgraduate degree from the London School of Economics in 1985. She is a member of the Nigeria Bar Association and the International Women Lawyers Association.

She is a renowned entrepreneur and a management and leadership development expert. She focuses in areas relating to business management, human resource management, education, problem solving and strategy development.

She started her career as a legal officer at the Legal Department of Nigeria Arab Bank and served as a Barrister at Sotimu & Co legal practitioners until 1987. After this period, since 1988, as the Chief Executive Officer, she has successfully established and managed over ten businesses which include the renowned Global International College ,Professional Business Center and Match Makers Job Center, Party Planners Nigeria Limited ,Global Educational Counseling, World of Fun, Global Bookshop, Business Mentoring Limited and British Education Consultants

DR EYITAYO OLUBUNMI ADEBIYI, is the Co-president of Jakinminis International Company Gte/ltd. He qualified as a Medical Doctor from the University of Lagos in 1998 and practiced for three years. His passion for humanitarian service led him into obtaining his masters of public health (MPH) from the same university of Lagos in 2004.

He started his career in public health as the South West Zonal Health Officer of the Nigerian Red Cross Society. After almost three years, he became the Edo zone Senior Medical Services Advisor on HIV/AIDS management for Family Health International (GHAIN) where he worked for four years before proceeding to Hygeia Foundation as part of the senior management team in 2008.

He obtained a second masters degree in Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Benin in 2008. With his experience in both local and international organizations, he facilitates in various public health workshops and seminars for companies and organizations. He is happily married to Olubukola Adebiyi.

MR KINGSLEY OMOSE is the Managing Partner of Reliance Partners, a firm of Legal practitioners based in Apapa, Lagos. In 1986, he graduated from University of Benin with a Bachelors degree in Law and from the Nigerian Law School in 1987.

He immediately started his career in Law at Shetimah Liberty & Co before proceeding 4yrs after to join Abogah & Co in 1990 where he worked for another 5yrs. Now with almost 10yrs experience in the law practice, he boldly stepped out in1995 to set up his own firm called Omose & Co which he managed diligently for 7yrs before establishing Reliance Partners in 2006.

Though with 22yrs working experience in the Law profession, he still writes as a hobby with a view to enhancing a common way of viewing, interpreting and relating to issues. His articles have been published in various National Dailies such as Businessday, Punch, The Sun, The Guardian etc. He is married to Olubunmi and they have 3 children.

MRS ADEBIYI OLUBUKOLA nee Akinlosotu obtained a B.Sc degree in Management and Accounting from Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU) in 2002. In 2008, she obtained an EDS Certificate in Entrepreneurial Management (CEM) from Pan African University.

She worked briefly in two organizations before launching off on her own on February 1st 2004 to start Just J Ventures, a supply company. Since October 2004, young but naturally endowed with a strong leadership and entrepreneurial spirit, her passion and huge burden for the less privileged led her into establishing and managing Jakinminis International Company limited by guarantee, where she serves as the President/Chief Executive Officer.
Her zeal and commitment earned her two humanitarian service awards in 2006 and recently, she won a one `year`Scholarship under the Goldman Sachs 10,000 women initiative to empower 10,000 women entrepreneurs in emerging and developing countries across the world. She is happily married to Dr Eyitayo Olubunmi Adebiyi.
